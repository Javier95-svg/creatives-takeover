## 🔑 Key SEO Strategy Changes

### 1. Keyword Targeting Shifts

**Previous Focus:**
- Generic "creative business"
- Broad "AI tools"
- "Startup planning"

**New Focus (Based on Analysis):**
- **Creative entrepreneur** (lower difficulty, higher intent)
- **Solopreneur** (untapped opportunity, specific audience)
- **Business planning tool** (product-led SEO)
- **AI business coach** (unique positioning)
- **Creative business validation** (long-tail, high intent)

### 2. Title Optimization Strategy

- Kept titles under 60 characters for optimal display
- Integrated primary keywords in first 50% of title
- Added unique value propositions (e.g., "Launch in 30 Days")
- Replaced generic terms with specific, searchable phrases

### 3. Description Optimization Strategy

- Kept descriptions under 160 characters
- Front-loaded with primary keywords
- Added semantic variations (e.g., "creative entrepreneurs and solopreneurs")
- Included action-oriented language ("Turn your ideas into...")
- Emphasized free value and specific outcomes

### 4. Heading Optimization (H1)

**Homepage H1 Change:**
- **Before:** "The Digital Partner for Building From Zero" (vague, no keywords)
- **After:** "AI Co-Founder for Creative Entrepreneurs" (clear keywords, unique positioning)

**Rationale:** 
- Integrates 2 primary keywords
- Owns unique positioning ("AI Co-Founder")
- Clear target audience ("Creative Entrepreneurs")
