## Credit Allocation Strategy

### How Credits Are Deducted

1. **Monthly Quota First**: Credits are deducted from your monthly quota first, then from your balance
2. **Tier-Based Allocation**: 
   - **Rookie**: 25 credits/month
   - **Rising**: 50 credits/month  
   - **Pro**: 150 credits/month
3. **Credit Reset**: Credits reset monthly at the start of each billing cycle

### Feature Access by Tier

#### Dashboard Features
- **Focus Funnel**: FREE (read-only for Rookie, full access for Rising+)
- **Decision Sprint**: Rising+ only (2 credits per generation)
- **Core Metrics**: FREE (read-only for Rookie, full access for Rising+)
- **Weekly Mission**: FREE (read-only for Rookie, full access for Rising+)
- **Your Tasks**: Rising+ only (FREE, no credits)
- **Roadmap Generation**: Rising+ only (5 credits)

## Notes

- **FREE features** remain accessible even when credits are exhausted
- **View limits** apply to VC Search based on subscription tier (not credit-based)
- **Pro-only features** require Pro subscription regardless of credits
- Credits reset monthly at the start of each billing cycle
- All AI-powered actions consume credits; browsing and viewing content is typically free
- **Read-only access** means you can view but not generate/modify content
- **Full access** means you can use all features within your credit limits
