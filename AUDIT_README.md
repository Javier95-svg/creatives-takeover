## 🎓 Key Concepts

### Current State
**Activity-Focused Dashboard**
- Tracks: daily activity, streaks, wins
- Answers: "What did I do today?"
- Good for: engagement, gamification
- Weak at: business intelligence

### Future State
**Business Growth Hub**
- Tracks: projects, metrics, readiness
- Answers: "How is my business progressing?"
- Good for: business outcomes, investment readiness
- Strong in: founder operating system

### Transformation Strategy
**Prioritize Business Intelligence**
- Focus on: real business outcomes
- Measure: progress toward investability
- Guide: clear path to fundraising
- Deliver: actionable insights

## ❓ FAQ

### Why these recommendations?
Based on founder needs: investors want metrics, validation, traction. Dashboard must show business progress, not just activity.

### Why quick wins first?
Fast value validates approach, builds momentum, and improves feedback before larger investments.

### Why investor focus?
Mission: turn ideas into investable businesses. Must support the full journey to funding.

### What about mobile?
Short-term: responsive web. Long-term: native apps or PWA.

### Timeline realistic?
Starts at 1–2 weeks; full vision is 3–6 months, with value every phase.
