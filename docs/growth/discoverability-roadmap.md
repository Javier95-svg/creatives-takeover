# Creatives Takeover Discoverability Roadmap

## Goal

Intercept early-stage founders while they are actively searching for answers across Google, Reddit, X, LinkedIn, Facebook groups, and YouTube. Route every useful discovery moment toward the lowest-friction activation path: **Build My ICP Free**.

## Core Funnel

1. Founder searches or asks a problem-specific question.
2. Creatives Takeover answers with a framework, checklist, teardown, or tutorial.
3. The CTA points to a specific next step, not a generic signup page.
4. Visitor completes the free ICP Builder.
5. Dashboard recommends the next journey step: Waitlist, PMF Lab, MVP Builder, GTM Strategist, or Insighta.

## SEO Clusters

| Cluster | Founder intent | Primary CTA |
| --- | --- | --- |
| ICP / Customer Clarity | "Who is my customer?" | `/icp-builder` |
| Validation | "Should I build this?" | `/waitlist`, `/pmf-lab` |
| Build | "What should my MVP include?" | `/mvp-builder`, `/tech-stack` |
| Launch / GTM | "How do I find users?" | `/go-to-market` |
| Fundraising | "Am I ready to raise?" | `/insighta`, `/pitch-deck-analyzer` |

## 30-Day Execution

### Growth Lead
- Build a keyword map of 50 founder-intent questions.
- Use UTM links for every external channel: `utm_source`, `utm_medium`, `utm_campaign`, and `utm_content`.
- Review weekly: organic sessions, referral sessions, signups, ICP completions, and Starter upgrades.

### Content Editor
- Publish and maintain the first answer library pages under `/answers`.
- Repurpose every answer page into:
  - 1 LinkedIn founder post.
  - 1 X thread.
  - 3 short community replies.
  - 1 future YouTube script when video capacity exists.

### Founder / Community Operator
- Prioritize `r/startups`, `r/Entrepreneur`, `r/SaaS`, `r/indiehackers`, `r/microsaas`, `r/SideProject`, `r/startup_ideas`, `r/growmybusiness`, `r/nocode`, `r/AlphaandBetausers`, and `r/roastmystartup`.
- Leave 20 high-quality Reddit replies per week.
- Run 2 non-promotional teardown posts after useful comment history exists.
- Join 10 relevant Facebook groups and comment for 2 weeks before posting.

### Engineer
- Keep `/answers` and answer pages in the sitemap.
- Keep tool pages internally linked to the answer library.
- Add dynamic story sitemap support when a build-time Supabase service role process is available.
- Ensure signup/ICP analytics preserve UTM attribution.

## Platform Playbooks

### Reddit
- Comment first, link later.
- Use founder/operator accounts, not a faceless brand account.
- Mention Creatives Takeover only when the tool directly helps finish the advice.
- Best reply format: diagnose the risk, give a 3-5 step framework, then offer the free ICP Builder only as an optional next step.

### X
- Daily: 5-10 thoughtful replies and 1 original founder lesson.
- Weekly: 1 teardown thread and 1 product walkthrough clip.
- Use concise copy and conversational tone. Avoid hashtag stuffing.

### LinkedIn
- Position as: "Creatives Takeover is an AI-powered startup development platform for first-time founders, connecting customer clarity, validation, MVP building, go-to-market execution, traction, and fundraising preparation in one guided system."
- Use founder personal profiles for reach and the company page as proof.
- Weekly: 3 founder posts, 1 company resource post, 10-15 thoughtful comments.

### Facebook Groups
- Use personal founder identity.
- Do not drop links unless allowed or requested.
- Offer checklists and mini-audits directly in the group.
- Ask admins before running teardown threads or Q&A posts.

### YouTube
- Start only if 4 tutorials per month are realistic.
- First videos:
  - Define your ICP in 10 minutes.
  - Waitlist vs MVP.
  - Validate your startup idea before building.
  - Build a GTM strategy for your first 100 users.
- Put "Build your ICP free" in the first line of the description and pinned comment.

## Success Metrics

- Organic clicks, impressions, average position, and indexed pages.
- Referral sessions from Reddit, X, LinkedIn, Facebook, and YouTube.
- Organic/social visitor to signup rate.
- Signup to ICP completion rate.
- ICP completion to first dashboard return rate.
- Rookie to Starter conversion from organic cohorts.
- Brand mentions and qualified community replies.

## Guardrails

- Do not use fake UGC, fake testimonials, or fabricated social proof.
- Do not spam communities or post links before trust exists.
- Do not send traffic to generic pricing unless the user is already comparing plans.
- Every external answer should be useful without requiring the reader to click.
